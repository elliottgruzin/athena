Who We Are

About Athena

Athena was founded by Trevor Gruzin, a former Accenture Senior Managing Director, and Elliot Gruzin, an AI Engineer — to bridge the gap between high-level strategy and hands-on execution.

We believe the best consulting outcomes come from pairing deep industry experience with modern, technology-driven approaches. Our team has delivered transformative work across financial services, technology, government, and startups.

Named after the Greek goddess of wisdom and strategic warfare, Athena embodies our philosophy: sharp thinking, decisive action, and outcomes that endure.
